Monitor your computing environment with machine generated music

Monitor your computing environment with machine generated music, Use Perl and fluidsynth to create a real-time musical composition of your system status. Learn how to integrate various system monitoring data into a harmony producing, midi controlled audio synthesis. Explore audible information methods and configurations to help you monitor and manage your computing environment. Local cached text Audio example with Hammered Instruments (think dulcimer, not intoxicated saxophone), another example with Electronic Sounds, and another with Simple Orchestral instruments.
